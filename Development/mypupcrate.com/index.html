PupCrate

Features

Sync

Backup your data and sync it across multiple devices using Dropbox or Google Drive.

Write

Write about your dreams. Keep a daily log. Inspire yourself.

Location

Automatically add location with your device's GPS or a custom place title.

Tags

Categorize your entries by adding tags to them.

Reminders

Create one-time or recurring reminders to encourage you to write in your journal.

Photos

Attach a photo to an entry to make the memory even better.

Markdown

Use markdown syntax to display beautifully formatted text.

Sharing

Share photos and text with Narrate for easy journal entries.

Day One

Seamlessly sync with your Day One journal through Dropbox.

Tasker

Automatically create new entries or launch the entry editor from Tasker.

Google Now

Easily create new journal entries using the Note to Self feature.

Automatic Backups

Automatically backup your journal to the SD card on your phone.
